## Overview

Slices wrap arrays in Go, and provide a more general, powerful, and convenient interface to data sequences. 
In this chapter, we will cover: 
- Creating and initializing of slices.
- Iteration of slices.
- How to grow a slice.
- Work with subsets of slices.
- Some slice tricks.
